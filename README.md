# SKETCHZ
During a 3-month project, 
I worked with a team to develop SKETCHZ, a multiplayer FPS in Unity. 
I implemented the complete weapon system, first-person view, weapon/UI systems, 
adaptive Scriptable Objects, loot and rarity mechanics, paint decals/VFX, team colors, 
bombs, healing, custom music integration, animations, and network-ready weapon data.
